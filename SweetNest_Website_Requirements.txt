MAJOR MODIFICATIONS NEEDED FOR THE WEBSITE
=============================================================

The original master prompt is very generic and corporate-focused.
For a Candy Buffet & Dessert Table Catering website, the design,
layout, sections, and branding must be modified heavily to match:

- Event catering industry
- Party aesthetics
- Luxury dessert presentation
- Family-friendly branding
- Wedding/event audience
